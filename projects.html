WINTER 2017 COLOR ME ZINE

COLOR ME zine, our first publication, revolved around our personal connections and interpretations of color.

we printed a limited run of 35 editions, not including copies to be given to the artists. we also offered digital copies of the zine.

we chose 14 artists, ranging in mediums from graphic design to fine art to photography.
