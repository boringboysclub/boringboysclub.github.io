about

BORING! BOYS! CLUB! is a collective and studio that revolves around collaboration, expression, and breaking boundaries.

we want to give underrepresented artists in all creative fields (fine arts, graphic design, music, etc.) a platform to showcase their work and meet possible collaborators in industries that heavily rely on the “it’s who you know that matters” mentality.

+ established in 2017 by kris o. and kelly w.
